Learn About Health

Nutrition

A balanced diet is essential for good health. It includes a variety of nutrients from different food groups. Focus on fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.

Key Nutrients: Vitamins, minerals, proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Tips for Healthy Eating:

  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Limit processed foods, sugary drinks, and unhealthy fats

Exercise

Regular physical activity is crucial for maintaining physical and mental health. It helps control weight, reduces the risk of chronic diseases, and improves mood.

Types of Exercise:

  • Cardiovascular exercises (running, swimming, cycling)
  • Strength training (lifting weights)
  • Flexibility exercises (yoga, stretching)

Recommended Amount: At least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week.

Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

Strategies for Maintaining Mental Health:

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Get enough sleep
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques

Hygiene

Good hygiene practices are essential for preventing the spread of germs and maintaining overall health.

Key Hygiene Practices:

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water
  • Brush your teeth twice a day
  • Shower or bathe regularly
  • Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing
